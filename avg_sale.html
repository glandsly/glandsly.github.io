Our Analysis

Through our analysis, we discovered that Travis County (Austin) has the highest average sale price over the years; compared to other counties in Texas. In 2019, the top five most expensive counties to purchase a home in include:

  1. Travis County
  2. Collin County
  3. Denton County
  4. Dallas County
  5. Midland County

In 2019, the five least expensive counties to purchase a home in include:

  1. Guadalupe County
  2. Bexar (San Antonio) County
  3. Kauffman County
  4. Ector County
  5. Johnson County
